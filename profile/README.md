# xAutomations

We ship AI products and automated workflows for businesses.

xAutomations is a product studio building fully automated micro-SaaS tools, AI workflows, and connected systems that help teams move faster, reduce manual work, and scale operations.

## What We Build

- **AI products** that turn business ideas into working software
- **Workflow automations** that connect tools, data, and teams
- **AI agents and assistants** for repetitive operational tasks
- **Dashboards and internal platforms** for visibility and control
- **Vertical SaaS tools** focused on practical business outcomes
